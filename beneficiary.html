Data Privacy & Terms of Service

Pursuant to the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), the City Government of Koronadal is committed to protecting your privacy. Please read and agree to the compliance statements for PESO and CSWDO.

PESO
Public Employment Service Office

Privacy Statement: PESO collects and processes your personal data (name, contact info, educational background, skills profile) to facilitate job matching, referrals to employers, livelihood training, and emergency employment programs. Your information is shared only with authorized partners in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Terms & Conditions: You certify that all profile information submitted is true and accurate. You consent to the matching of your qualifications with job openings and training opportunities.

CSWDO
City Social Welfare and Development Office

Privacy Statement: CSWDO processes sensitive personal information (household income, medical records, vulnerability indicators) to evaluate eligibility for financial aid (AICS), emergency relief, and other social welfare assistance programs. Strict confidentiality is maintained for all case files.

Terms & Conditions: You certify that all statements regarding income and household status are accurate. You consent to validation reviews by social workers. Fraudulent statements will disqualify you from receiving assistance.
